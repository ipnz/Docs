### MEETING PROCEEDINGS
1.  **Welcome & Apologies:** Record attendees and apologies.
2.  **Confirmation of Quorum:** The Chairperson confirms a quorum is present.
3.  **Chairperson's Explanation:** The Chairperson explains the purpose of the meeting and the legal requirement to adopt a new constitution for reregistration.
4.  **Discussion:** Members are invited to ask questions or discuss the proposed constitution.
5.  **Resolution Put:** The Chairperson formally puts the special resolution to the vote.
